FAQ

Questions, gently answered.

Is Lunara a medical app? +

No. Lunara is an educational and lifestyle companion. For any medical concern, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Is my data private? +

Your data is encrypted and never sold. You control what's shared — including partner insights, which are fully optional.

Does it work without a regular cycle? +

Yes. Lunara adapts to irregular cycles, hormonal contraception, PCOS, perimenopause and more.
