Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

Lunara is an AI-powered female health companion. We are committed to protecting your personal and health data with the highest standards of care, fully in line with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. What data we collect

  • Account information (email address, name)
  • Health and cycle data you choose to log (period dates, mood, energy, symptoms)
  • Conversation content with Lunara AI
  • Device and usage data (browser type, app interactions)

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and improve the Lunara service
  • To personalise your AI companion experience
  • To send you product updates and relevant health insights (with your consent)
  • We never sell your data to third parties. Ever.

4. Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with row-level security encryption. Health data is treated as sensitive personal data under GDPR Article 9.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or permanently delete all your personal data at any time.

6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions: privacy@lunara.app

FAQ

Questions, gently answered.

Is Lunara a medical app?+

No. Lunara is an educational and lifestyle companion. For any medical concern, please consult a qualified healthcare professional. We take this distinction seriously and make it clear throughout the app.

Is my data private and secure?+

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted, stored securely, and never sold to third parties. You can export or permanently delete all your data at any time. We are fully GDPR and Swiss nFADP compliant.

Does it work without a regular cycle?+

Yes. Lunara adapts to irregular cycles, hormonal contraception, PCOS, perimenopause and more. Your experience is always personalised to your actual situation, not an average.

How is Lunara different from other period trackers?+

Lunara goes far beyond tracking. It combines cycle awareness with an empathetic AI companion, hormone education, and personalised daily guidance — all in one calm, beautifully designed space.

Where can I learn more about specific health conditions?+

Our Health Library covers eight conditions in depth — from PCOS and endometriosis to thyroid health and menopause — with science-backed information and questions to bring to your doctor.
