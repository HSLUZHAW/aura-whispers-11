FAQ

Questions, gently answered.

Is Lunara a medical app?+

No. Lunara is an educational and lifestyle companion. For any medical concern, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Is my data private?+

Your data is encrypted and never sold. You control what is shared, including partner insights, which are fully optional.

Does it work without a regular cycle?+

Yes. Lunara adapts to irregular cycles, hormonal contraception, PCOS, perimenopause and more.

When is the app launching?+

We are rolling out access gradually. Join the waitlist for early invites and founding-member pricing.

Where can I learn more about specific health conditions?+

Our Health Library covers eight conditions in depth, from PCOS and endometriosis to thyroid health and menopause, with science-backed information and questions to bring to your doctor.

Privacy Policy

Your data is yours

Lunara is built on the belief that health data is deeply personal. We collect only what is needed to provide the service, and we never sell or share your data with third parties for advertising.

What we collect

We collect the information you provide directly, such as your email address when you join the waitlist and any cycle or health data you enter within the app. We also collect basic usage data to understand how the app is being used so we can improve it.

How we use it

Your health data is used solely to power your personal experience within Lunara. It is never used to train AI models without your explicit consent, and it is never shared with employers, insurers, or other third parties.

Cookies

We use essential cookies that are necessary for the app to function. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.
